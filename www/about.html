About us

Clinicom incorporated reputable and best-trained surgeons and specialists. Our belief is to assist providing high-quality services with competitive affordable costs by qualified medical teams, at advanced hospitals, and with cutting-edge technologies that will guarantee the best results for all patients. All of the treatments are performed at internationally accredited hospitals. All the equipment used; the doctors, caretakers, staff, and general hospital conditions and all of the processes are inspected and authorized by international medical bodies and the Oman Ministry of Health.


Clinicom services span all across Oman, through out all 11 governorates. From Dhofar all the way up to Musandam with around the clock 24 hours service.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve the health and well-being of the people we serve by ensuring highly effective, community-based support and care

Our Vision

Our vision for us Clinicom is to strive to be the best health care center for any health/medical needs. Providing multiple health services and care with utmost satisfaction.
